Research Interest

I am working on spectroscopy of organic semiconductors under the supervision of Dr. Jayeeta Bhattacharyya. I am interested in studying the nature of exciton and carrier dynamics in organic semiconductors. I am using electromodulation spectroscopy technique to study these systems.
